Customer Data Dictionary — Reference Document
=============================================

This document describes the fields used in the customer database.

CUSTOMER IDENTIFIERS
--------------------
customer_id: A unique alphanumeric identifier assigned to each customer at registration.
Format: C followed by 3 digits (e.g. C001). This is the primary key across all systems.
Never reused after account deletion.

PERSONAL INFORMATION
--------------------
first_name, last_name: Customer's legal name as provided at signup. 
Not validated against government ID. May contain Unicode characters for international customers.

email: Primary contact email. Must be unique across all accounts. Used for login and notifications.
Some enterprise accounts may have no email if SSO is configured (nullable).

phone: International format with country code. Used for 2FA and support contact.
Format varies by country. Not guaranteed to be unique (family plans may share a number).

date_of_birth: Used for age verification and GDPR compliance. 
Customers must be 18+ to hold a premium or enterprise plan.
Format: YYYY-MM-DD. PII field — handle with care.

ACCOUNT INFORMATION
-------------------
signup_date: UTC date when the account was created. Immutable after creation.

last_login: UTC datetime of the most recent authenticated session.
Null if the customer has never logged in after email verification.

is_active: Boolean flag. False means account is suspended or voluntarily deactivated.
Inactive accounts retain data for 90 days before purging per GDPR policy.

country: ISO 3166-1 alpha-2 or full country name. Determines applicable tax rates
and data residency requirements (EU vs non-EU).

SUBSCRIPTION AND BILLING
-------------------------
subscription_plan: One of four tiers:
  - basic      ($9.99/month)  — limited to 3 users, 10GB storage
  - standard   ($49.99/month) — up to 10 users, 100GB storage
  - premium    ($149.99/month)— up to 50 users, 1TB storage
  - enterprise ($499.99/month)— unlimited users, dedicated support

monthly_spend: Actual billed amount in USD for the current billing cycle.
May differ from plan price due to add-ons, discounts, or proration.
Enterprise customers may have custom pricing — treat as sensitive financial data.

DATA QUALITY NOTES
------------------
- email is nullable for enterprise SSO accounts (about 2% of accounts)
- phone numbers are not standardized — some include dashes, spaces, or parentheses
- country field was added in 2020; records before that may have NULL country
- is_active was backfilled in 2021 — all pre-2021 records default to true
